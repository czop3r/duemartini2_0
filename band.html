A duet of young and ambitious opera singers Marcin Czopka (PL) and Martin Koza (SK). They are graduates of the AMKL Art College in Wroclaw (PL). Their skills are being improved with the excellent singers from different parts of Europe. The duemartini repertoire especially contains canzonets, not only in the traditional version (including piano or orchestra), but as well arranging for a jazz band, showing new innovate look. The old-italy songs combined with modern jazz band sounds gives new and fresh perspective around Neapolitan music.
